About Me

Hello! I'm Zayyan, a 13-year-old coding enthusiast with a passion for technology and a love for bringing ideas to life through code. I embarked on my coding journey at the tender age of 7, and my adventure began with HTML, the fundamental language of the web.

As I delved into the world of web development, I quickly embraced CSS3 to bring style and design to my web projects. The ability to create visually appealing and interactive websites fascinated me, and I was hooked from the start.

Coding isn't just a hobby for me; it's a creative outlet and a way to express my ideas. I'm always excited to dive into new coding projects during my free time. Whether I'm building a sleek website, automating tasks with Python, or exploring the world of server-side scripting with Node.js, I find joy in every line of code I write.

My journey has exposed me to a range of programming languages and technologies, including HTML, CSS, Python, and Node.js. Each language and tool has expanded my skillset and allowed me to tackle various coding challenges.

I'm continually learning, experimenting, and growing as a coder, and I can't wait to see where my coding journey takes me in the future. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see some of the exciting projects I've been working on. If you'd like to collaborate or discuss coding, please don't hesitate to reach out. Let's code and create something amazing together!

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy exploring my coding world.
